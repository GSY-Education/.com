About Us

Learn about our history, vision and mission

Our History

The Great School of Yemen / formerly Yemeni-British School (GSY) provides both the British National Curriculum and the curricula of the Yemeni Ministry of Education. It was established as a leading educational institution aimed at providing high-quality education that combines authentic Arab heritage with modern educational curricula.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Yemen and the region, providing excellent education that contributes to preparing creative and innovative generations capable of leading sustainable development and serving society effectively and professionally.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing a stimulating and creative educational environment that relies on the latest teaching methods and modern technologies, focusing on developing the student's personality in all academic, social and moral aspects.
